Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

At GTNJ Studios, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our applications.

Our Core Privacy Commitment

We do not store any of your personal images or data in our cloud storage. Your privacy is our top priority, and we have designed our applications with a minimalist approach to data collection.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Login Information (Limited Collection)

The only personal data we store is your Google or Apple email ID used for login. This information is necessary for:

  • Account management and authentication
  • Syncing your preferences across devices
  • Providing customer support when needed
  • Managing in-app purchases and credits

1.2 Data We Do NOT Collect or Store

  • Personal images or photos
  • User-generated content (stories, notes, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (beyond what's necessary for app functionality)
  • Browsing history or usage patterns for advertising purposes
  • Contacts or calendar information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 No Monetization of User Data

We do not use your data for advertisements.
We never sell your data to third parties.

2.2 Legitimate Use of Login Data

Your email address is used solely for:

  • Account authentication and security
  • Sending important service updates and notifications
  • Processing account deletion requests
  • Managing subscription and payment information

3. Third-Party AI Services

3.1 Google Gemini AI Integration

All generative AI functionality in our applications relies exclusively on Google Gemini generative APIs. When you use AI-powered features:

  • Your prompts and requests are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing
  • The generated content is returned to your device
  • We do not store the AI-generated content on our servers
  • Google's data processing is governed by their privacy policies

Important: Third-Party Privacy Policy

Google Gemini API services are subject to Google's privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy practices:

Google Privacy Policy

Google Gemini API Terms of Service

4. User Consent and Choice

4.1 Notification and Consent

Upon first launching any of our applications, you will be notified of these privacy policies and data practices. You have the option to:

  • Accept - Proceed with using the app under these terms
  • Decline - Choose not to use the app if you do not agree with our policies

4.2 Opt-Out Options

If at any point you wish to stop using our services and have your account data removed, you can submit an account deletion request through our Account Deletion page.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your email address and account information:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure Firebase Authentication for login management
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to user data within our organization

5.2 Data Retention

Your email address is retained only as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your email is permanently removed from our active database and marked to prevent future use.

6. Children's Privacy

While some of our apps (such as BedTime Story Maker) are designed for family use, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents and guardians are responsible for supervising their children's use of our applications.

7. International Data Transfers

Our services use Firebase (a Google service), which may process and store data in various locations worldwide. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your email address to these locations, subject to the protections described in this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the app or via email
  • Provide a reasonable time period for you to review changes before they take effect

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access - Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Objection - Object to certain data processing activities

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: gtnjstudios@gmail.com
Company: GTNJ Studios

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with applicable data protection laws and regulations. We are committed to complying with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant privacy regulations.

Summary: Our Privacy Commitment

  • We only store your email address for login purposes
  • We do not store personal images or user-generated content
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • AI processing is handled by Google Gemini APIs
  • You can delete your account at any time
